**<span style="font-size: 24px">Quotient Group and Homomorphisms.</span>**
- Definition of groups
- Group is a disjoint union of cosets
- $G/N$ is a group iff $N\trianglelefteq G$
- The natural projectino from $G$ onto $G/N$ is a homomorphism
- If $G/Z(G)$ is cyclic, then $G$ is abelian
- Isomorphism theorems

**<span style="font-size: 24px">Direct and Semidirect Products.</span>**  
- if $H, K\trianglelefteq G$ and $H\cap K=1$, then $HK\trianglelefteq G$ and $HK\cong H\times K$
- if $H\trianglelefteq G, K\leq G$ and $H\cap K=1$, then $HK\trianglelefteq G$ and $HK\cong H\rtimes_\varphi K$ for some homomorphism $\varphi  : K\rightarrow Aut(H)$
- $H\times K\cong H\rtimes_\varphi K$ iff $\varphi (K)=1$ iff $K\trianglelefteq H\rtimes_\varphi K$
